Watch Piplup in Pokémon the Series on Pokémon TV

See the star of Pokémon GO’s January Community Day in action by tuning in to classic episodes on Pokémon TV.

The Water-type Pokémon Piplup is the star of Pokémon GO's January Community Day, and players will have the opportunity to catch dozens of the adorable little Pokémon. To help get in the mood for the veritable Piplup party, you can relive some of Piplup's most exciting Pokémon the Series moments on Pokémon TV. As Dawn's Pokémon partner, you'll see Piplup face all sorts of challenges as it travels alongside Ash, Pikachu, and all their friends.

Make like a Bubble Beam and pop on over to Pokémon TV for the next two weeks to see Piplup in action in classic episodes of Pokémon the Series! Remember that you can watch Pokémon TV here on Pokemon.com or using the Pokémon TV mobile app.
